# Content Pillars

## Resources
- Basic: Wood, stone, food.
- Rare: Gold, diamond, amethyst, reidite, emerald.
- Biomes: Forest (basic), Winter (diamonds), Lava (reidite), Desert (emeralds).

## Recipes
- Tools: Pickaxes, swords (tiered).
- Structures: Walls, farms.
- Consumables: Cooked food, potions.

## Mobs
- Passive: Animals for drops.
- Aggressive: Wolves, spiders, dragons.
- AI: Patrol, chase players.

## Progression
- Tech tiers: Unlock recipes via points.
- Optional: Cosmetics, unlocks.
