# Game Design

## Core Game Mechanics

### Survival Systems
- **Hunger**: Decreases over time, requires food
- **Health**: Damage from combat, environment, or starvation
- **Temperature**: Affected by weather, clothing, and shelter
- **Stamina**: Consumed by actions, regenerates with rest

### Resource Gathering
- **Wood**: From trees and wooden structures
- **Stone**: From rocks and mountains
- **Food**: From hunting, farming, and foraging
- **Fiber**: From plants and animals
- **Metal**: From mining and smelting

### Crafting System
- **Basic Tools**: Stone axe, wooden pickaxe
- **Advanced Tools**: Metal tools, weapons
- **Building Materials**: Walls, floors, roofs
- **Structures**: Workbenches, furnaces, storage

### Building System
- **Foundation**: Required for all structures
- **Walls**: Protection from elements and enemies
- **Doors**: Access control and security
- **Furniture**: Storage, crafting stations, decoration

## World Design

### Biomes
- **Forest**: Abundant wood and wildlife
- **Desert**: Scarcity of water, temperature extremes
- **Snow**: Cold environment, requires warm clothing
- **Plains**: Flat terrain, good for building
- **Mountains**: Rich in stone and metal ores

### Environmental Factors
- **Day/Night Cycle**: Affects visibility and temperature
- **Weather System**: Rain, snow, wind, storms
- **Seasons**: Impact resource availability
- **Natural Disasters**: Rare events that challenge players

## Multiplayer Features

### Player Interaction
- **Chat**: Local and global chat channels
- **Trading**: Item exchange between players
- **Teams**: Cooperative gameplay mechanics
- **PvP**: Combat in designated zones

### Social Systems
- **Friends List**: Track and join friends
- **Clans/Guilds**: Organized player groups
- **Leaderboards**: Competition and achievements
- **Events**: Special multiplayer activities
