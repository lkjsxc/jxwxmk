# Rewards & Notifications

## Stat Increases
Achievements are not just cosmetic; they provide permanent stat boosts.

### Boost Types
- **Max HP**: Increased survivability.
- **Move Speed**: Faster travel.
- **Damage**: Increased combat effectiveness.
- **Gather Speed**: Faster resource collection.
- **Craft Speed**: Reduced crafting time.

### Scaling
- **Tier 1 Achievements**: +1% to related stat.
- **Tier 2 Achievements**: +2% to related stat.
- **Tier 3 Achievements**: +5% to related stat.

## Notifications
### HUD Display
- **Location**: Centered horizontally, immediately below the player's HP bar / HUD.
- **Duration**: 3 seconds.
- **Animation**: Fade in (0.2s), Sustain (2.6s), Fade out (0.2s).
- **Content**: Achievement Icon + Title + "Unlocked!".

### Menu
- A dedicated "Achievements" tab in the main menu.
- Lists all achievements: Locked (grayed out) vs Unlocked (colored).
- Shows progress bars for tracked achievements (e.g., 45/100 Mobs Killed).
