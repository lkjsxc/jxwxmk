# Achievement List (Implemented)

## Exploration
- **Novice Walker**: Walk 1,000 steps.
- **Marathon Runner**: Walk 100,000 steps.

## Combat
- **First Blood**: Kill 1 mob.
- **Monster Hunter**: Kill 100 mobs.
- **Slayer**: Kill 1,000 mobs.
- **Seasoned Veteran**: Tool level 5.
- **Legendary Smith**: Tool level 10.

## Gathering
- **Lumberjack**: Gather 100 resources.
- **Deforestation**: Gather 1,000 resources.
- **Miner**: Gather 100 resources.
- **Deep Driller**: Gather 1,000 resources.
- **Resource Tycoon**: Gather 5,000 resources.

## Crafting + Building
- **Apprentice Smith**: Craft 10 items.
- **Master Smith**: Craft 1,000 items.
- **Builder**: Place 50 structures.
- **Architect**: Place 500 structures.

## Special
- **Pacifist**: Walk 5,000 steps with 0 kills.
