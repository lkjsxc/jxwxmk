# Achievement List

## Exploration
- **Novice Walker**: Walk 1,000 steps.
- **Marathon Runner**: Walk 100,000 steps.
- **Globetrotter**: Visit every biome.
- **Cartographer**: Reveal 80% of the map.

## Combat
- **First Blood**: Defeat your first mob.
- **Monster Hunter**: Defeat 100 mobs.
- **Slayer**: Defeat 1,000 mobs.
- **Survivor**: Survive with < 5% HP.
- **Unstoppable**: Kill 10 mobs without taking damage.

## Gathering
- **Lumberjack**: Chop 100 trees.
- **Deforestation**: Chop 1,000 trees.
- **Miner**: Mine 100 rocks.
- **Deep Driller**: Mine 1,000 rocks.
- **Gatherer**: Collect 500 resource items.

## Crafting
- **Apprentice Smith**: Craft 10 items.
- **Master Smith**: Craft 1,000 items.
- **Builder**: Place 50 structures.
- **Architect**: Place 500 structures.

## Survival
- **Survivor I**: Survive 1 day.
- **Survivor X**: Survive 10 days.
- **Survivor C**: Survive 100 days.
- **Glutton**: Eat 500 food items.

## Wealth
- **Hoarder**: Have 1,000 items in inventory/storage.
- **Rich**: Collect 1,000 gold (if applicable).
