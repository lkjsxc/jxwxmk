# Achievement List (Target)

## Variety Targets

- 10x increase in achievement variety versus baseline.
- 100+ achievements across exploration, combat, crafting, and social play.

## Exploration
- **First Steps**: Walk 1,000 steps.
- **Trailblazer**: Walk 25,000 steps.
- **Marathoner**: Walk 100,000 steps.
- **Cartographer**: Discover 5 biomes.
- **Pathfinder**: Discover 10 biomes.
- **Skyline**: Reach highest alpine peak.

## Survival
- **Warmth Seeker**: Survive 1 night in tundra.
- **Heatproof**: Survive 1 day in desert.
- **Iron Stomach**: Eat 100 meals.
- **Hydrated**: Drink 100 times (if thirst enabled).
- **Resilient**: Survive 10 hazard events.

## Gathering
- **Lumberjack I**: Gather 100 wood.
- **Lumberjack II**: Gather 1,000 wood.
- **Lumberjack III**: Gather 10,000 wood.
- **Quarry I**: Gather 100 stone.
- **Quarry II**: Gather 1,000 stone.
- **Quarry III**: Gather 10,000 stone.
- **Prospector I**: Mine 50 ore nodes.
- **Prospector II**: Mine 500 ore nodes.
- **Prospector III**: Mine 2,500 ore nodes.

## Crafting + Building
- **Apprentice**: Craft 25 items.
- **Journeyman**: Craft 250 items.
- **Master**: Craft 2,500 items.
- **Builder I**: Place 25 structures.
- **Builder II**: Place 250 structures.
- **Builder III**: Place 1,000 structures.

## Combat
- **First Blood**: Defeat 1 hostile mob.
- **Hunter**: Defeat 100 mobs.
- **Slayer**: Defeat 1,000 mobs.
- **Elite Bane**: Defeat 25 elite mobs.
- **Boss Breaker**: Defeat 5 world bosses.
- **Defender**: Win 10 settlement defenses.

## Weapons Mastery
- **Blade Adept**: Reach sword mastery 10.
- **Axe Adept**: Reach axe mastery 10.
- **Bow Adept**: Reach bow mastery 10.
- **Crossbow Adept**: Reach crossbow mastery 10.
- **Staff Adept**: Reach staff mastery 10.

## Economy
- **Trader**: Complete 25 NPC trades.
- **Merchant**: Complete 250 NPC trades.
- **Caravan Guard**: Escort 10 caravans.
- **High Roller**: Spend 10,000 coins.
- **Entrepreneur**: Earn 10,000 coins.

## Social
- **Party Starter**: Complete 10 party quests.
- **Guilded**: Join a guild.
- **Guild Captain**: Lead 25 guild missions.
- **Community Pillar**: Donate 10,000 items to guild storage.

## Settlement
- **Resident**: Bind to a settlement core.
- **Builder of Towns**: Repair 100 settlement structures.
- **Guardian**: Help repel 5 settlement invasions.
- **Diplomat**: Reach reputation tier 3 in 3 settlements.

## Events
- **Storm Chaser**: Participate in 10 world events.
- **Festival Goer**: Complete 10 seasonal quests.
- **Relic Hunter**: Claim 10 event relics.

## Progression
- **Tier One**: Reach player level 10.
- **Tier Two**: Reach player level 25.
- **Tier Three**: Reach player level 50.
- **Legendary**: Reach player level 80.
