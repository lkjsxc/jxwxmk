# Plants + Food

Plants are tiered by biome and used for food, alchemy, and trade.

- **Berry Bush (L1)**: meadow, basic food.
- **Wheat (L1)**: plains, bread base.
- **Herbs (L2)**: forest, basic potions.
- **Mushrooms (L2)**: swamp, poison/antidote.
- **Cactus Fruit (L2)**: desert, thirst relief.
- **Spice Bloom (L3)**: savanna, trade goods.
- **Frost Lotus (L4)**: tundra, cold resistance.
- **Sun Orchid (L5)**: desert, heat resistance.
- **Nightshade (L6)**: swamp, toxins.
- **Celestial Herb (L8)**: event-gated, high-tier potions.

## Level Rules

- Plant nodes have shorter respawn timers than ores.
- Rare plants spawn near world events and settlements.
