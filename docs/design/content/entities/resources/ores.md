# Ores

Ore nodes drive tiered crafting and weapon progression.

- **Copper (L2)**: starter metal.
- **Tin (L2)**: alloying.
- **Iron (L3)**: core metal tier.
- **Silver (L4)**: tools and trade.
- **Gold (L4)**: economy and prestige.
- **Platinum (L5)**: advanced gear.
- **Mithril (L6)**: elite weapons.
- **Obsidian Ore (L7)**: volcanic tier.
- **Aetherium (L8)**: event-gated alloy.
- **Dragonsteel (L10)**: boss-tier metal.

## Level Rules

- Ore nodes require matching tool tier to harvest.
- Smelting is required before crafting most metal items.
