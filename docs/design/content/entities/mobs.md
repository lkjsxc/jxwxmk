# Mobs

AI-controlled entities.

## Passive
- **Rabbit**: 
    - HP: 10.
    - Behavior: Flees player.
    - Drop: Meat.

## Aggressive
- **Wolf**:
    - HP: 50.
    - Damage: 10.
    - Behavior: Chases player within radius.
    - Drop: Fur, Meat.
- **Bear**:
    - HP: 200.
    - Damage: 30.
    - Behavior: Guards territory.
    - Drop: Fur, Bone.
