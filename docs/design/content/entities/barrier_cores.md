# Barrier Cores

A Barrier Core is the physical entity that generates a protective barrier.

## Properties
- `id`: Unique identifier.
- `x`, `y`: Position in the world.
- `level`: The level of the core, determining its barrier range.
- `base_range`: The starting range of the barrier at level 1.

## Interaction
Players can interact with Barrier Cores to see their level and range. (Future expansion: upgrades).
