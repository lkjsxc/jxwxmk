# Biomes

Zones with distinct environmental properties.

## Forest
- **Temp**: Neutral (20°C).
- **Resources**: Abundant Wood, Berry Bushes.
- **Mobs**: Wolf, Rabbit.
- **Color**: Green (#3a3).

## Winter
- **Temp**: Cold (-20°C).
- **Resources**: Sparse Wood, Gold.
- **Mobs**: Polar Bear (Aggressive).
- **Color**: White (#eee).
- **Effect**: Rapid Freezing without Fur.

## Desert
- **Temp**: Hot (40°C).
- **Resources**: Cactus (Food/Damage), Stone.
- **Mobs**: Scorpion.
- **Color**: Sand (#edc).
