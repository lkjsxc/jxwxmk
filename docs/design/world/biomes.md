# Biomes

Biomes define climate, hazards, resource tiers, and creature distribution.

## Biome Catalog (Baseline)

- **Meadow**: mild temperature, low threats, starter resources.
- **Forest**: dense wood, mixed wildlife, moderate cold at night.
- **Boreal**: conifers, fur-bearing animals, slow freezing.
- **Tundra**: extreme cold, rare herbs, high-end furs and ice ore.
- **Alpine**: steep terrain, thin air, high-tier ores and gems.
- **Desert**: heat and thirst pressure, cactus food, glass sand, scorpions.
- **Savanna**: open grasslands, herd animals, fast predators.
- **Swamp**: poison hazards, mushrooms, rare alchemy plants.
- **Jungle**: dense foliage, high humidity, exotic wood and venom.
- **Coast**: fish resources, salt, shipwreck loot.
- **Volcanic**: lava hazards, obsidian, fire-resistant mobs.

## Biome Elements

- **Weather**: rain, snow, heat waves, sandstorms, fog.
- **Terrain modifiers**: movement penalties, visibility changes.
- **Localized POIs**: ruins, shrines, lairs, resource clusters.
- **Biome-specific crafting**: recipes unlock from biome materials.
